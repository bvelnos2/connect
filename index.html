Windows and Phones

1

Add Our Server

Navigate from the "Worlds" category to "Servers." Scroll all the way down the server list until you see the "Add Server" button then click it.
2

Join Polaris Lazarus

Enter our server information:
Server Name: Polaris Lazarus
IP Address: 158.69.52.208
Port Number: 25594
3

Time to Play

Now you're good to go. Our server should always be available in the "Servers" tab! Good luck out there.

Xbox and Switch

Changing the DNS of your console is harmless. It won't change your console experience in any way.
(click here if you have an IPv6 internet connection)

1

Open Device Settings

Navigate to the network settings tab inside your console settings.
2

Change Your DNS

Select your DNS and change it to manual. Enter the code 104.238.130.180 for primary and 8.8.8.8 as the secondary. Restart your console just to make sure these settings take effect.
3

Join Polaris Lazarus

Launch Minecraft on your system and head over to the "Servers" tab. Join any of the existing servers and you'll be taken to a special screen. From here you can add our server!

Server Name: Polaris Lazarus
IP Address: 158.69.52.208
Port Number: 25594
4

Time to Play

Now you're good to go. Our server should always be available in this special server screen! Good luck out there.
